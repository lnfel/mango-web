We’re a web design company that makes your product stand out

In a world where companies compete for customer loyalty, it’s essential to create a product that can solve a user’s problem better than the competition. Mango System is a web design company that helps you create products that make a difference for users.

How? We take a research-driven approach to product design. This lets us understand your business idea and explore the best ways to realize it. Through user experience research and testing, we get a 360-degree view of your users’ problems so we can come up with the best solution.

Research-driven design results in the right product decisions

Our product design approach lets us provide you with insights about your product and your users. Here’s what research-driven design means to us as a professional web design company.

UX design is an exploration

We extensively research the problem you’re striving to solve. We examine your competitors and the market. And most importantly, we analyze user feedback. We listen to your users all the time. Permanent exploration helps us detect what needs improvement and make changes early on. This saves you time and money in the long run.

We design journeys, not screens

Before drawing screens, we create user flows. These flows, or journeys, show pain points, expectations, and goals that users want to achieve with your product. All this data helps us provide relevant user experiences and consistent design across all devices that a user interacts with. Our goal is to delight your users so they return to your product.

Good design is invisible

Design is a language. Good design should be understandable to all. We create experiences that don’t require sophisticated onboarding. Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines and Google’s Material Design Guidelines help us do that. We stick to best practices of user experience and user interface design to create intuitive user interactions.

Design goes with technology

Design and technology have a common goal: to create a great experience for the end user. RubyGarage is a web design and development company where web designers and software engineers work as one team – and are located in one office. This collaboration results in amazing products that give our clients the best of both worlds.
